About IEEE Gaza Initiative :

Is a collaboration between the IEEE in Islamic University of Gaza and IEEE in Tafila Technical University in Jordan. This initiative aims to highlight the role of the people of Gaza in scientific matters and showcase their academic contributions. Additionally, it seeks to reignite and restore education in Gaza after its complete halt due to the war, bringing hope and progress to the educational sector for the people of Gaza.

What can you benefit from this initiative?

1- Freelance job opportunities for Gaza residents will be available on the Jobs page.


2- Can benefit from the scientific research published by Gaza residents on IEEE Xplore, and access an electronic library, workshops, and courses in various fields, all available on the Knowledge page.
